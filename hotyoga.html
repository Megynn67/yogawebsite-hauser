Hot Yoga Resources

These are just a few of the studios where I've enjoyed practicing yoga, and felt a sense of community.

  • Bikram Yoga - actually, no. Skip this one. Watch the documentary about Bikram.
  • Yoga Pod Fort Collins, Colorado - all levels of warm and hot yoga, plus fitness and restorative classes.
  • Hot Yoga Downtown in Albuquerque, New Mexico - hot Vinyasa, workshops, and a lovely community.
  • Praxis Yoga in Colorado Springs, Colorado - traditional 26/2 hot yoga, warm vinyasa, power yoga, and workshops.
